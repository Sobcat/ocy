FOSHAN OUCHUANGYUAN MACHINERY MANUFACTURE CO.,LTD.

Guangdong  Foshan Ouchuangyuan Machinery Manufacturing  Co.,  Ltd.  has been committed to the research  and  development, production, sales and after-sales technical  service of  household paper machine,  provides their customers practical and stable household papermachine, assortative equipment, and excellent after-sales service. The company founded in the 1990 s, after yearsof development and management, Ouchu-

angyuan machinery got sustainable  development, has begun  to take shape and has a certaininfluence in the in the industry of post processing for

household paper machinery.

      Ouchuangyuan machinery is insisting on the tenet of working  together, profession and win-win, taking customer's demand as  the  guidance, 

actively explore innovation,  improve the  process and structure,  strict on quality control,  to provide customers  with professional and humanized 

mechanical equipment.

      The products developed by ourselves mainly are small  roll / roll towel folders, slitting machines, rewinders (roll paper), slitting machine (with 

sawing machine), pocket handkerchief folder, napkin folders (square napkin folders),nonwoven folders,boxed towel folders,boxed sealer, packing 

machine, and beauty makeup towel folders etc.To develop  equipments for the customers in  special  specification  and  requirement, we are well 

received  by customers. To meet the higher requirements  of  customers, won the  customers'  trust  and support,  we  keep  in close contact with 

customers, obtain information from the production front line,walk ahead of customers,and have

technical  improvements  in  advance according to the  market  development.  This  is  also  the  motive force that Ouchuangyuan is survived and 

developing.
